Elevator to Space

Intro

The space elevator is just a concept, for now. It's just a big elevator that goes all the way to space. Building a space elevator has many benefits, but we still have many problems to solve before we can build one.

Problems

There are many problems that need solving before we can build a space elevator

  1. We need a material strong enough to withstand the forces of gravity
  2. We need a way to protect the elevator from space debris
  3. We need to find an efficient way to build it

Benefits

There are many benefits that come with a space elevator

  1. Cheaper space travel(Less rocket fuel needed)
  2. Regular people like you or me can go to space(provided you have money)
  3. More satellites(better communication)
  4. Less space junk
  5. More jobs for everyone
  6. We will be able to show Flat Earthers that the Earth is round
